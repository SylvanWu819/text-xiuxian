```

  ↓ postMessage
扩展 (extension.ts)
  ↓ 处理消息
游戏引擎 (GameEngine)
  ↓ 更新状态
扩展 (extension.ts)
  ↓ postMessage
前端 (main.js)
  ↓ 渲染UI
```

### 9.2 道具数据流
```
事件定义 (events.json)
  ↓ itemChanges
GameEngine.applyEffects()
  ↓ 
ResourceManager.addItem()
  ↓
playerState.resources.items
  ↓
extension.ts (序列化)
  ↓
main.js (渲染)
  ↓
UIRenderer.renderStats()
  ↓
背包UI显示
```

### 9.3 存档数据流
```
GameEngine.playerState
  ↓
SaveSystem.serializePlayerState()
  ↓ items: Array.from(Map)
VSCode globalState
  ↓
SaveSystem.deserializePlayerState()
  ↓ items: new Map(Array)
GameEngine.playerState
```
